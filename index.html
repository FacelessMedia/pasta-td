📖 How to Play Pasta TD

1

🍳 Place Kitchen Tools

Click a tool in the left shop, then click an empty tile on the kitchen floor to build it. Each tool costs gold (🪙).

2

🌊 Start a Wave

Hit ▶ Start Wave to send pasta down the path. Defeat them all before they reach the dining room!

3

🔧 Upgrade Tools

Click a placed tool to open the upgrade panel. Spend gold on damage, range, fire rate, splash, or sell for 70% back.

4

🌳 Skill Tree

Earn 🥫 Marinara Marks by completing waves. Spend in the Skill Tree on Tomato (offense), Garlic (economy), or Basil (defense) branches.

5

✨ Prestige

Reach wave 5+ and Prestige to reset your run for 🍅 Sauce Points — permanent currency for game-changing perks.

6

🍅 Synergies

Place a 🧄 Garlic Press next to your towers to buff their damage. Use 🧂 Salt Shaker to slow fast enemies before your big hitters finish them.
